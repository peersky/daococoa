## [vote4best.app](https://vote4best.app)

[vote4best.app](https://vote4best.app) is a novel "**vote for fun**" and "**vote to earn**" approach to bring mass usage of based blockchain voting trough simple and gamified manner to end, non technical users.

Besides solving adaption problem it also aims to solve some fundamental DAO security risks and issues.

And is also something fun to play for me and my friends
